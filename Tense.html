Tense :

Tense is basically related with the person & the work which is going on in our present time,
was going on in the previous time or will go in future.
                                                                             Here, the person is important who is
doing the work.

For Example : Ram is going to market.
Note : Where Ram is important who is doing the work.

Basically there are three types of Tenses :

* Present Tense :

Present tense is related with the work which is going on in our present time.

For example : Ram goes to school.
Present tense is further divided into four types :-

  • Present Indefinite / Simple.
  • Present Continuous.
  • Present Perfect.
  • Present Perfect Continuous.

* Past Tense :

Past tense is related with the work which was going on in our previous time.

For example : Ram went to school.
Past tense is further divided into four types :-

  • Past Indefinite / Simple.
  • Past Continuous.
  • Past Perfect.
  • Past Perfect Continuous.

* Future Tense :

Future tense is related with the work which will go in our coming time.

For example : Ram will go to school.
Future tense is further divided into four types :-

  • Future Indefinite / Simple.
  • Future Continuous.
  • Future Perfect.
  • Future Perfect Continuous.
